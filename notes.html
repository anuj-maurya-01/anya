Dear My Love 💕

Every heartbeat writes your name on the pages of my soul. This diary holds not just words, but the moments that made me fall deeper in love with you.

From the way your eyes smile before your lips do — to how you make silence feel like music.

Chapter 1 — First Sparks ✨

It all began with a glance that felt like a lifetime. You didn’t just walk into my life — you danced in, with laughter that rewrote my story.

I still remember how my heart forgot to beat for a moment, just to listen to yours.

Chapter 2 — The Little Things 💗

The late-night talks, the teasing, the shared dreams — every piece of us stitched together this beautiful chaos called “us”.

Maybe forever isn’t measured in years — but in every time I whisper your name and smile.

Made with ❤️ for our eternal love. Forever and always.
